I am a second year student at the University of California Santa Cruz majoring in Computer Engineering with a focus on system programming.

  • UCSC SHPE Outreach Chair

Im really interested in human interaction with hardware and softwares. I became interested in digital hardware after seeing the impact to information humans retain through technology. In my free time I like to work on robots projects such as coding in C++ and smaller raspberry pi coding projects.

I am also really passionate about the society of hispanic professional engineers, I currently serve as the Social Chair of the UCSC She chapter leading events for other Latin students in STEM.

I have also worked with Twitter as part of their early bird program where I strengthen my soft and technical skills. I believe I can use my past experiences to grow in any opportunity given to me.
